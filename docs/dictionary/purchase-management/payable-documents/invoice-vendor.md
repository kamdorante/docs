# Documentos por Pagar

## Descripción

La ventana permite desplegar y entrar facturas para un proveedor. Facturas de proveedores pueden ser generadas desde una orden de compra o desde un recibo de envíos.

## Acceso

Menú: Gestión de Compras → Documentos por Pagar → Documentos por Pagar

## Pestañas

### Invoice

The Invoice Tab defines the parameters of an Invoice generated by a Business Partner.  It constitutes an accounts payable.

### Invoice Line

The Invoice Line Tab defines the individual items or charges on an Invoice.

### Allocate Invoice

Pestaña Allocate Invoice.

### Invoice Tax

The Invoice Tax Tab displays the total tax due based on the Invoice Lines.


### Payment Schedule

Invoice Payment Schedule

### Accrual Setup

Accrual Setup

### Allocation

Allocation of the Invoice to Payments or Cash
